Academy of Engineering

Here at Mallard Creek High, we pride ourselves in having a strong PLTW program. We have a wide variety of courses, ranging from Civil Engineering to Digital Electronics.

Here's a closer look at the Academy of Engineering at Mallard Creek:

IED: Introduction to Engineering Design
  • Designed for 9th-10th grade students
  • Introduces students to various engineering concepts and ideas, such as technical sketching and CAD modeling
  • Comprehension and application of the design process is critical, and is heavily used
POE: Principals of Engineering
  • Designed for 10th-11th grade students
  • Exposes to students to major concepts all over various types of engineering
  • Employs problem-solving, research and design
DE: Digital Electronics
  • Designed for 10th-11th grade students
  • Includes extensive studies on the foundation for all things electronic in the modern world
  • Introduces students to combinational and sequential logic, engineering standards, and technical documentation
CEA: Civil Engineering and Architecture
  • Designed for 11-12th grade students
  • Apply previously acquired skills in civil engineering and architecture to designing structural properties
  • Problem solving is emphasized, as well as an ability to communicate with professionals of the trade
Software and Tools Involved
  • Autodesk Auto-CAD
  • Autodesk Inventor
  • Autodesk Revit
  • VEX Hardware

Our Academy of Engineering program is preparing our students for the future, but also spreading awareness about engineering and engineering-related fields to the rest of the student body. Check out some pictures of our AoE in action!
