Home

Mallard Creek High School is a public high school (part of the Charlotte Mecklenburg Schools district) that serves students grades 9 through 12 in Charlotte, North Carolina. We are the largest school in our district, with a size over 351,000 square feet. Mallard Creek has many talented sports teams in football and basketball, as well as diverse clubs and groups, such as TSA and DECA.

Our Technology Club

Our Technology Club is actually a conglomerate of three clubs: Technology Student Association, VEX Robotics, and Maverick Motorsports. This site is meant to serve the purposes of all three organizations, as well as our Academy of Engineering, the home of PLTW courses at Mallard Creek. We're paving the way for tomorrow with these organizations, preparing to tackle the troubles of tomorrow.

CTE at Mallard Creek

Here at Mallard Creek, we take Career and Technical Education very seriously. We have a diverse selection of CTE courses to choose from, including:

  • STEM Courses (Follow the PLTW curriculum)
  • Business and Marketing Courses
  • Game Art Design

We believe that CTE education is vital to the future of our society, because these courses teach the skills and ethics to survive in the world of tomorrow. Behind these classrooms, not only is there learning taking place, but inspiration and innovation.

For more information on the STEM courses at Mallard Creek, visit our Academy of Engineering section.
