Local Solutions to E-waste


The state of North Carolina has a very important program for e-waste. This program divides recycling responsibilities between manufacturers, retailers, local government, and consumers. This program includes the introduction of a law that bans the discarding of televisions, computers, printers, and computer accessories in landfills. (N.C. General Statutes 130A-309.130 through 130A-309.141 "Discarded Computer Equipment and Television Management"). This law is extremely beneficial because it strongly encourages everyone who deals with electronics to properly recycle them.




Additionally, there are several programs statewide and nationwide that can help you you to properly recycle e-waste. These include:


  • Sims Recycling
  • All Green Recycling
  • Electronic Recyclers
